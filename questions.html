Objective type printing questions with explanations.

Q1. Offset printing is also known as:

(A) Direct printing process
(B) Relief printing process
(C) Indirect lithographic printing process
(D) Intaglio printing process
Correct Answer: (C)

Explanation:
Offset printing transfers ink from plate → blanket → paper, hence it is an indirect lithographic process.

Why others are wrong:
(A) Direct printing prints plate directly on paper.
(B) Relief printing uses raised image areas.
(D) Intaglio printing uses recessed image areas.

Q2. Gum Arabic is used in fountain solution to:

(A) Increase ink tack
(B) Act as preservative
(C) Desensitize non-image area
(D) Reduce surface tension
Correct Answer: (C)

Explanation:
Gum Arabic keeps non-image areas hydrophilic and ink-repellent.

Why others are wrong:
(A) Ink tack is controlled by varnishes.
(B) Preservatives are biocides/fungicides.
(D) Surface tension is reduced by alcohol.
